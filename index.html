OnFoot Privacy Policy

Walk Safe, Walk Smart

Last updated: December 29, 2024

Welcome to OnFoot! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our pedestrian navigation and safety app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture (optional)

1.2 Location Data

Important: Location data is essential for OnFoot to function. We collect your location to:

  • Provide walking navigation
  • Show nearby safety reports
  • Allow you to report safety issues at your current location

1.3 Safety Reports

When you create a safety report, we store:

  • Report type (dangerous area, construction, poor lighting)
  • Location coordinates
  • Timestamp
  • Your user ID (to allow you to manage your reports)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Navigation: To provide accurate walking directions
  • Safety Features: To display community-reported safety information
  • App Improvement: To understand how users interact with the app
  • Account Management: To maintain your profile and saved data

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

Your data may be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • Safety Reports: Report locations are visible to all users (without personal identification)
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for secure data storage and Mapbox for maps
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Cloud database with encryption at rest
  • HTTPS: All data transmitted is encrypted
  • Row Level Security: You can only access and delete your own data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Delete: Remove your safety reports at any time
  • Account Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account
  • Opt-out: Disable location services (note: this will limit app functionality)

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Safety reports may be automatically removed after a period of time to keep information current
  • Navigation history is not permanently stored

7. Children's Privacy

OnFoot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Third-Party Services

OnFoot uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Mapbox: For maps and navigation (Mapbox Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For data storage (Supabase Privacy Policy)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
